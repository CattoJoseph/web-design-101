La Tour Eiffel

Paris, France

Constructed 1887-1889

See the construction of the Eiffel Tower. The first floor was completed on the 1st April 1888. The second floor was completed on the 14th August 1888. The top section was completed on the 31st March 1889. The tower has 1665 steps. 2.5 million rivets were used 7,300 tonnes of iron 60 tonnes of paint.
